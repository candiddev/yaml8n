## Open Source

YAML8n is licensed under the [GNU AGPL](https://www.gnu.org/licenses/agpl-3.0.en.html).  Additionally, the code YAML8n generates is covered under the AGPL.

## Exemptions

Companies interested in using YAML8n without the AGPL copyleft requirements can purchase a license exemption.  We sell exemptions to individual companies using Stripe.  You will be emailed a license exemption after checkout.
